Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, commonly known as "Straw Hat Luffy" or simply "Straw Hat", is the founder, captain, and strongest combatant of the increasingly infamous and powerful Straw Hat Pirates.

Kaido

Kaidou of the Beasts, renowned as the world's "Strongest Creature",is the Governor-General of the Beasts Pirates and was formerly one of the Four Emperors that ruled over the New World.
